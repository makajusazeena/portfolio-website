kasa boxing

Kasa Boxing is a one-page boxing club website designed to showcase the gym’s energy, services, and training programs. Built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and powered by WordPress Content Management System. The site features a detailed services section covering boxing fundamentals, strength training, and conditioning, along with a pricing section displaying membership plans. An embedded video offers a glimpse into the gym’s dynamic environment, while a contact form seamlessly integrates with email for quick inquiries and bookings. This project delivers a professional and engaging online presence for Kasa Boxing.
